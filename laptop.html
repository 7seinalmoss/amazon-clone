Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra

About the Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra

The Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra is the latest flagship smartphone featuring a stunning 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor, up to 16GB RAM, and a massive 200MP camera. It's a powerhouse for professionals and tech enthusiasts alike.

Specifications

  • Display: 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X, 120Hz
  • Processor: Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 / Exynos 2400
  • Camera: 200MP main, 12MP ultra-wide, 10MP telephoto
  • Battery: 5000mAh, 45W fast charging
  • OS: Android 14 with One UI 6
